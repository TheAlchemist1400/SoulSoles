About Us



Welcome to SoulSoles.co — South Africa’s trusted destination for exclusive universal sneaker drops.
Founded to bridge the gap between local demand and global sneaker culture, SoulSoles.co brings the world’s most sought-after sneakers directly to South African buyers.
All our pairs are sourced directly from abroad — including rare, limited-edition releases — and shipped with two delivery options: Standard and Premium.
We know international sneaker buying can feel risky. That’s why SoulSoles.co is partnered with an independent escrow financial service — your payment is securely held until your order is confirmed delivered.
Whether you're a collector, reseller, or style enthusiast, we provide peace of mind every step of the way.

We understand that trust is everything , espically when orders take time to arrive

How it works

  • When you order, your payment is held securely by our escrow services.
  • As the shipping process commences you will receive a tracking number, to keep track of your order
  • Once your order is successfully delivered and confirmed, then only we will receive the payment
  • In-case of issues occuring, you can log an appeal, and a refund will be issued after the review though they can be lodged once the product has been delivered

Sourcing from us

In order to meet the diverse needs of customers in owning Authentic shoes, clothes, and accessories from famous brands.

With 3 years of prestige and seniority in this field, we guarantee Auth Standard Products - Fastest Order Time - Best Order Price!

  • Navigate to sourcing page

  • We will get back to you by email, so be sure to check it within the next 48 hours
